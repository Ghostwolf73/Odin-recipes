Chicken

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Chicken
  • Garlic salt
  • Oil
  • Garlic
  • paprika
  • Eggs

How to:

  1. Boil chicken
  2. Add flour, paprika, garlic salt to a dish
  3. Beat eggs, add water, salt and flour
  4. Dip chicken in egg mixture and place in flour mixture
  5. Heat oil to 350℃
  6. Fry the chicken pieces until skin is golden brown
  7. Drain on paper towels
  8. Enjoy!
