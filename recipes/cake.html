Cake

Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Strawberries
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Baking powder

How to:

  1. Preheat oven. Grease and flour three 9-in. round baking pans.
  2. Add cream,sugar, eggs and strawberries to a bowl
  3. Beat together and add baking powder
  4. Transfer to pan
  5. Bake until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean
  6. Wait 22-26 minutes
  7. Cool for 10 minutes
  8. Enjoy!
